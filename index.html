    Interesting Facts

    1.Jagannath Temple:Defying Nature's Code of Conduct

    It looks like the flag mounted on the top of the Jagannath Temple is a unique flag. This particular flag flows in the opposite direction to the wind's course without any scientific background to back it up.

    2.Veerabhadra Temple: See The Hanging Pillar

    The mind-boggling part of this temple is one of these pillars. In opposition to any other pillar, one pillar dangles from the roof, which means it does not touch the ground. It leaves a recognizable space for visitors to test it with a cloth.

    3.Padmanabhaswamy Temple: Mystery behind the closed chamber

    The temple has 7 chambers, of which only 6 were opened and wealth worth 22billion was found. The last chamber that still remains closed has no latch or bolt to it, and is rumoured to hold the greatest treasure in the world.

    4.Konark Temple: The wheels of time

    The main attraction of the temple is its twelve pairs of wheels located at the base of the temple. These wheels are not ordinary wheels but tell time as well as the spokes of the wheels create a sundial. One can calculate the precise time of the day by just looking at the shadow cast by these spokes.

    5.Kal Bhairav Nath Temple: Alcohol as offering

    Believe it or not, the only offering that are made to God here is alcohol,be it whiskey or wine. Alcohol is directly poured into the deity's open mouth and the same is offered to devotees as prasad.
